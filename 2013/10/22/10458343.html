When people understand how important it is to localize their App, everybody wins!

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2013/10/22, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2013/10/22/10458343.aspx

One of best moments someone in the World-Readiness business can have is when someone really gets it.

They get how important it is to localize something if you want to see it picked up by more people around the world.

And they choose to blog about it!

Like Bernardo Zamora did in the Windows Phone blog, with Tips for localizing and monetizing your app for Windows Phone users.

With all of the data to prove how much wider the reach your App will have if you localize into more languages, and hit more markets, reach more customers.

I love it when the message gets out there, when people have the opportunity to see the benefits of localization in such clear and unambiguous ways.... 😏😏😏
