  • Lepcha 0x1C00
  • Ol Chiki 0x1C50
  • Cyrillic Extended-A 0x2DE0
  • Vai 0xA500
  • Cyrillic Extended-B 0xA640
  • Saurashtra 0xA880
  • Kayah Li 0xA900
  • Rejang 0xA930
  • Cham 0xAA00
  • Ancient Symbols 0x10190
  • Phaistos Disc 0x101D0
  • Lycian 0x10280
  • Carian 0x102A0
  • Lydian 0x10920
  • Mahjong Tiles 0x1F000
