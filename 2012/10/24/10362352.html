Where the hell is Michael?

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2012/10/24 07:01 -04:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2012/10/24/10362352.aspx

It all started on the night of Sunday, October 14th.

I fell.

Hardly unusual for me, but this fall was quite different than the 983 falls prior.

There was a pop.

Unable to get up, I called 911.

The ambulance took me to Overlake, where xrays quickly confirmed that I seriously broke my left hip.

Surgery to replace the hip happened the next day (Monday, October Th).

Surgery went very well, and I 100% pain free.

I left the hospital on Thursday, October 18th, to a rehab in Shoreline named Crista Assisted Living

People nearby want to visit? It's 19303 Fremont Avenue North.

You may want to call first (my cellular is 425-260-8744) if you're planning on visiting!

Lots of physical therapy, lots of occupational therapy, lots of dealing with six weeks of range of motion restrictions which, if violated, could result in me re-breaking the hip.

I really don't want to do that.

So, for now, I work through my MS, my post-op restrictions, and integrating the iBOT in this community where the next youngest patient is 25 years older than me,

I am working remotely. I'll get into blogging again.

And today, I'll be doing my three presentations at the 36th Internationalization and Unicode Conference remotely from my room. :-)
