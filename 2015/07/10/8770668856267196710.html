There are three kinds of irony in the literary sense (replace 'I' below with 'the hero' since the literary hero on this Blog is literally I, by which I mean me:

• Dramatic irony: when everyone knows something that I do not;

• Situational irony: when something happens but I was expecting the opposite to occur;

• Verbal irony: when I am saying one (of two) things but in actuality meaning the other.
