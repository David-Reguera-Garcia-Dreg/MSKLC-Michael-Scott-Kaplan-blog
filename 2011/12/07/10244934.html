I was in danger of losing some vacation time.

And therefore, to avoid that fate, I am going to take some time off.

Basically from now until the end of the year.

Now don't worry -- I have some blogs in the hopper.

So I'll appear to be writing, still.

Anticipating a response from management: my biweekly status will be ON VACATION.
