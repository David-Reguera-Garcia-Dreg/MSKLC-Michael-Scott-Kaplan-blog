Virtual Aimee Mann appearances

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2006/12/13 02:56 -05:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2006/12/12/1271250.aspx

When Aimee Mann is going to be doing a performance on television or online, I tend to get a whole bunch of mail from people who want to make sure I am being kept informed. I thought I'd make sure everyone knew about some of these upcoming events....

WOXY.COM: Celebrate the Holidays with Aimee Mann!

In the spirit of the season, on Wednesday, 12/13 at 3:00 pm EST/12:00 pm PST, the WOXY HOLIDAY MIXER will air Aimee Mann's First Annual Christmas Show recorded live from Bimbo's 365 Club in San Francisco. Aimee and her band performed a host of holiday themed songs including originals like: "Calling On Mary," and classics such as: "Winter Wonderland" and "You're a Mean One Mr. Grinch." Tune in and hear special guests join Aimee including: actor John C. Reilly and comedian Paul F. Tompkins.

After the completion of the initial broadcast on the WOXY Holiday Mixer, the show will be streaming for free through the end of 2006 at lala.com!

Tune in tomorrow at 3pm eastern for the show. Click here to access the WOXY HOLIDAY MIXER!

Late Night with Conan O'Brien, Thursday 12/14 @12:37PM

Keep in mind this is the "Wednesday night" show, which starts at 12:37am on Thursday.

There are some other shows that have already happening or will be happening like on KCRW or the World Cafe.

And for those who can make her Albany show at The Egg on Thursday night (Dec. 14th), she is appearing with the very talented Rachel Yamagata.

Unfortunately, this is not a Seattle show. Curses, foiled again!

In any case, enjoy! :-)
