I missed a 'case' pun....

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2006/08/21 03:01 -04:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2006/08/21/707828.aspx

Damn.

You know, if you read here regularly, that I am no stranger to making puns out of the word case.

But I never used the one that Mark Liberman mentioned on Language Log in his post Just in case.

Which is a little embarrassing as I actually knew about it.

I have a friend in England who mentioned it to me in email after they read this post of mine, and I recall thinking that might be interesting to work into a post at some point with a clever title with a pun attached to it, before I proceeded to forget about it entirely forget about it until I read Mark's post.

Damn. I need to keep better track of random things that occur to me throughout the day related to blog posts. :-)
