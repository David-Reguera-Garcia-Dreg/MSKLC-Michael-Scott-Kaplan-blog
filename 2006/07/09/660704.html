by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2006/07/09 14:33 -04:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2006/07/09/660704.aspx

And, as I predicted, it is Brittany and Juli in the finale this afternoon.... :-)

Brittany Lincicome was up by one on the 19th hole -- which means one hole of sudden death, beating out Lorena Ochoa.

Juli Inkster, on the other hand was up by four on the 15th -- which means with three holes left there was no way for Paula Creamer to win.

So it is Inkster and Brittany in the finals. I still have no idea why I am enjoying this so much, but it's Sunday so I'm just gonna go with it.... :-)
