My Left Ventricular Ejection Fraction (LVEF) is still about the same, which means my heart is still in the right place. :-)

Anyway, this means that soon I'll be ready for more Novantrone -- infusion #4, this time. Things seem to be going nicely still, not much else to report on the MS front. This is generally a good thing -- an area where no news can definitely be thought of as good news....
