My apparent obsession with "case" puns

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2005/01/16 22:20 -05:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2005/01/16/354210.aspx

I am sorry, but for reasons that I cannot explain I seem unable to make a posting about case without involving a pun in the title of the posting. To wit:

Get off my [lower] case! (or: Casing, the 1st)

The [Upper]Case of the Turkish Ä° (or: Casing, the 2nd)

How [case-]insensitive (apologies to Frank Sinatra)

Now sometimes I come up with good puns, but you would not know if by reading these! All three of them rank among the dumbest I have ever come up with, which is probably saying something.

I'll try to resist the temptation the next time I talk about case....

And truly apologies to Frank Sinatra. His song "How Insensitive" certainly deserves better than this.
