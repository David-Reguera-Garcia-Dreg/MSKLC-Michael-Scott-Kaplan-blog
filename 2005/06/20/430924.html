Jim Boggia in Seattle, July 13th

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2005/06/20 22:16 -04:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2005/06/20/430924.aspx

(nothing technical, yada yada yada...)

I mentioned him back when I was explaining what was Now Playing. An excellent singer/songwriter who has been everywhere worth being and then some. He is (along with Ben Arnold, Scott Bricklin and Joseph Parsons) 4 Way Street, and also has an excellent set of solo releases. And I have been able to see him perform several times -- in Portland, Philadelphia, and other places.

He is... Jim Boggia.

And on Wednesday July 13th, at the Showbox (in the Green Room!), Square Peg Concerts will present Jim Boggia with Kristin Connell.

Just $6.00 in advance at TicketsWest, and $7 day of show and at the door. Doors open at 8pm. You must be 21 or older....

A highly recommended show that I am not going to miss.

And if you are interested in going, then let me know. Maybe we can have some sort of a meetup of people who blog who like good music! :-)
