Doing a globalization conference again? Kewl!

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2005/11/14 19:10 -05:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2005/11/14/492750.aspx

If you were one of the people who was at the Microsoft Global Development and Deployment Conference (GDDC) in February of 2004, you know that a great conference with a lot of good information happened, and there was not even a major product released that we were talking about!

Well, hang on to your hats because Dr. International is starting to post about another conference some time of 2006 -- which means there will be a whole lot of new products and new international issues to learn about.

I'll submit ideas for talks on a whole bunch of the issues I like to cover here, and you should try and get your ideas in, too.

Check it out here and be sure to send in your ideas.

And if there are talks you want me to talk about, then post ideas here. :-)
