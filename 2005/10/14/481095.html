Let there be LIPs

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2005/10/14 08:31 -07:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/michkap/archive/2005/10/14/481095.aspx

Many years ago I saw the Rocky Horror Picture Show entirely too many times at the now-closed Coventry theatre (I think it was in Mayfield Heights?).

I still remember the very beginning of the audience participation:

We chant "Lips...lips...lips..." and cheer when they appear on the screen. Then we say:

{ "A long long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away,
God said: 'Let there be lips,' and there were.
And they were good. and the lips said 'thank you'. Sing!"

They looked like this:

It is amazing how over 20 years later I can be doing the same chant. But it is hard not to with the list of LIP languages increasing regularly. Now including the Konkani LIP!

Let's all chant together now.... Lips... lips.... lips.... :-)
