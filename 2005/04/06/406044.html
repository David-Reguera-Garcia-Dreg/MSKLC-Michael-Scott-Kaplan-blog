Michael Penn at the Tractor Tavern on April 9th

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2005/04/06 20:24 -04:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2005/04/06/406044.aspx

I usually don't do this sort of thing, but it is really good music. :-)

Singer/songwriter Michael Penn (husband to singer/songwriter Aimee Mann) will be at the Tractor Tavern in Ballard on April 9th (Saturday).

Highly recommended, but only if you like good, smart music.

Michael's new album (Mr. Hollywood Jr., 1947) will be out soon. And you will be able to hear a bunch of the new songs on Saturday.

Links for info on: Michael Penn and the Tractor Tavern.
