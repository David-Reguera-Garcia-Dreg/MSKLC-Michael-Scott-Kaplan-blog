New experiment on the comments issue

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2005/04/22 10:53 -04:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2005/04/22/410772.aspx

New experiment in blogging -- I am going to try only moderating anonymous comments for a bit for new posts for a while and see if I can hit the right balance between blocking spam and not making people wait for their comments to show up.

It will only happen for new posts, as Community Server has no "follow the global setting" feature that lets me change every post. I knew that "posting too much" thing was going to come back and bite me at some point. :-)

But if you want to try it out, just register and then we will know if it works or not!
