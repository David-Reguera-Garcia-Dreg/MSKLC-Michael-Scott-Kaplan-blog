Two Birds of a Feather Sessions proposed for TechEd 2005

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2005/04/04 20:17 -04:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2005/04/04/405363.aspx

Ok, people pointed out to me that both MS Employees and TechEd speakers can propose Birds of a Feather sessions.

Those who are interested and wanted to vote on the sessions can do so at BOF Voting.

The sessions I proposed are entitled:

  • Security and Unicode on Microsoft products

  • Collation and Security -- The Problems No One Noticed

If you are attending and believe either or both of them would hold interest, feel free to vote on them. I can promise some interesting monents as some people try and figure out how to get home early to fix up potential problems in their code....

Or you can propose your own BoaF talks here!
