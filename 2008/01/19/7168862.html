For all the 'Michael watchers' out there who were curious about that India trip....

I will be presenting an extended version of my recent Sorting It All Out - Even More Words on Collation talk that I did at the last Internationalization and Unicode Conference in San Jose, California.

The extended version contains additional Indic language examples as well as some general on the technical approach and how it comes out of the language itself, with several examples.

It is happening on January 26th, 2008 at 4PM at B.NET - the Bangalore .NET User Group, and this special version of the talk may never be seen outside of India, so I highly recommend it to anyone who would like to see me speaking (or blathering?) passionately about an interesting topic.... :-)
