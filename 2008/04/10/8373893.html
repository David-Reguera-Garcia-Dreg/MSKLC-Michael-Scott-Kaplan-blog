Yet another reason to cut out the noise

A friend of mine was looking at the headphones on my head when she walked up to me.

She shook her head.

"You do realize what a dork you look like wearing those, don't you?" she asked.

"And how" I asked "is this different from the way I look without headphones?"

"Hmmm. I guess it isn't."

And people wonder why I want noise-canceling headphones? There is obviously lots of noise I have to cancel out!
