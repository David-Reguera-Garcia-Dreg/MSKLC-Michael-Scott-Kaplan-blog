I'd be up anyway, I guess?

by Michael S. Kaplan, published on 2007/12/20 13:20 -05:00, original URI: http://blogs.msdn.com/b/michkap/archive/2007/12/20/6819613.aspx

I don't think my body is used to vacations.

It has been a few days now, and I just haven't been tired. It's not like I am doing much (just reading, watching movies, catching up on other people's blogs, browsing the web, etc.) but it just occurred to me that I haven't slept since Sunday night.

I honestly haven't been tired.

Very strange -- it isn't like insomnia (which I've had before so I know what it's like), and it's not like I used to sleep all that much anyway.

But for some strange reason, I just have been doing some kind of strange 24-hour thing.

Probably good I am on vacation, come to think of it -- since I am salary and not hourly, Microsoft would just get way too much benefit out of this without having to compensate me! :-)

Anyway, I'll figure it out.

But if I don't and you start seeing a ton of posts, you'll know why -- it's because I was up anyway....
